Understanding Leadership vs. Management

and Why Each Matters

Module Objectives

  1. Describe the role leaders played in historical study and how this information was shared through the ages.
  2. Provide examples of Management and Leadership behaviors according to Kotter and will be able to share two examples of why it is important to have a balance of both management and leadership within an organization.
  3. Provide a working definition of “leadership” based on the definition provided in Northouse and will be able to break down and explain the definition in terms of four components.
  4. Explain the basis of leadership trait research, Skill Theory, and will be able to name one strength and one criticism of each.
  5. Outline the connection between task and relationship in leadership styles based on the work of Blake and Mouton as outlined in Northouse.
  6. Define transformational leadership using the Four I’s of Transformational Leadership according to Bass and Burns as outlined in Northouse.
  7. Explain three critical steps to developing leadership skills according to Sashkin and Sashkin.
  8. Build on what they learned today in order to further develop the learners’ leadership.
