Understanding Leadership vs. Management

Topic 1: Brief History of Leadership Study

Objectives

  1. Describe the role leaders played in historical study.
  2. Share the role of storytelling in the study of leaders across history.
  3. Outline the three focus areas of modern leadership study from 1910-present.

Leadership

Leadership has been around for a very long time- in fact it is one of the oldest occupations and yet study of leadership is relatively new. The term “Leader” appears as early as 1300 but the term “leadership” appears much later in the 19th century

Leadership Phases

  • Trait period from about 1910-WWII
    • High level summary: focused on identification of traits- qualities and characteristics of great leaders. Sometimes known as Great Man theory.
  • Behavior period from WWII to the late 1960’s
    • High level summary: focused on what great leaders do/leadership action and behavior
  • Contingency Period from late 1960’s to present
    • High level summary: matching the leader to the situation- led to a variety of approaches to leadership and prescriptions for how to lead
